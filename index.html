Robot Design and Build Project:

Programming a PIC microcontroller in C to get the robot to follow a black line (University Project)
➢ I used SparkFun infra-red sensors to detect a black line.
➢ I used soldering to connect the electronic components with the microcontroller.
The university provided us with soldering equipment in labs and I managed to design the circuit and solder it to the circuit board.
➢ I used the concept of feedback control systems in programming for the robot to follow the black line. There were 8 sensors on the front of the robot. I managed to program the robot to keep the black line between el 4th and the 5th sensor so that the black line is at the centre of the robot. As the black line make any turns, the robot must follow. I managed to achieve that by calculating error which is distance between centre of the robot and the black line and then change robot's direction towards black line and multiply original speed of motor by error to increase the robot speed. As the robot goes further away from the line, robot's speed must increase in the opposite direction.
